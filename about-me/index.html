About Me

Get to know me better

Hi! I'm Aimar Goñi Benito

I'm 21 years old and from Vitoria-Gasteiz in the Basque Country, Spain. I'm a passionate game developer specializing in AI, gameplay, and graphics programming.

My journey with video games began as a child, creating small games that sparked my passion for development. This early interest led me to study at ESAT in 2021, where I've honed my skills with engines like Unreal Engine and Unity.

One of my proudest achievements is developing my own graphics engine using DirectX11 and OpenGL. This challenging project deepened my understanding of graphics programming and technical problem-solving.

I'm particularly fascinated by AI development, creating intelligent agents and simulations that bring virtual worlds to life. Whether it's NPC behaviors or evolutionary algorithms, I love exploring how AI can enhance gameplay experiences.

I'm constantly seeking new challenges to expand my skills and contribute to the evolution of game development. My goal is to create immersive, technically impressive experiences that push boundaries.
