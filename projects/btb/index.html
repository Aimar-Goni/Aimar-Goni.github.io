Beat The Beat

UnityVR

VR Unity game developed for the Meta Quest 2 VR Headset.

This project was developed by Carlos Cordero and myself (3 months | March 2023 - May 2023).

In this pseudo-rhythm game, the player has to hit the different balls thrown at him. The bounces off the walls and timing of the ball match the music of each level- This project focused on enhancing the game feel and responsiveness of the experience as much as possible, making good use of haptics, snappy particles, and good sound design and making the game as intuitive to pick up and play as possible.
