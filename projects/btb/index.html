Beat The Beat

Unity VR

VR rhythm game for Meta Quest 2

Project Overview

VR Unity game developed for the Meta Quest 2 VR Headset. This project was developed by Carlos Cordero and myself (3 months | March 2023 - May 2023).

In this pseudo-rhythm game, the player has to hit the different balls thrown at them. The bounces off the walls and timing of the balls match the music of each level.

Development Focus

This project focused on enhancing the game feel and responsiveness of the experience as much as possible, making good use of:

  • Haptics - Controller feedback for hits and misses
  • Visual Effects - Snappy particles for impact feedback
  • Sound Design - Rhythmic audio cues matching gameplay
  • Intuitive Controls - Easy to pick up and play

My Contributions

  • Implemented core ball mechanics and physics
  • Developed UI/UX systems for VR interaction
  • Optimized performance for Meta Quest 2
  • Created particle effects and visual feedback
  • Designed levels and gameplay flow
