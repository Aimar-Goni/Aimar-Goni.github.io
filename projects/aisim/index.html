AI Nature Simulation

Unreal Engine C++ AI

Evolutionary algorithms in a virtual ecosystem

Project Overview

A nature simulation made with Unreal Engine, as part of the HND AI course in Computer Science. The simulation creates a virtual environment where animals with genetic traits compete for survival, demonstrating evolutionary principles through emergent behaviors.

Ecosystem Dynamics

The simulation features a complex virtual ecosystem where:

  • Each animal has unique genetic traits that determine its characteristics and behavior
  • Creatures must find food and water while avoiding predators
  • Successful animals reproduce, passing on their genetic advantages
  • Populations evolve traits better suited to their environment over generations

Technical Implementation

  • Genetic Algorithm - Traits are inherited with possible mutations
  • Need-Based AI - Animals prioritize actions based on hunger, thirst, and energy
  • Behavior Trees - Complex decision-making through Unreal's AI tools
  • Data Tracking - Generational statistics and trait evolution monitoring

My Contributions

In this project, I developed:

  • Core animal behaviors (movement, eating, drinking, reproduction)
  • Evolutionary algorithm for trait inheritance and mutation
  • Fitness function to evaluate organism success
  • Visual dressing and animation implementation
  • Environment interaction systems
