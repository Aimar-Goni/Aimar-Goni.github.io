AI Simulation

Unity

A nature simulation made with Unreal Engine, as part of the HND AI course in Computer Science. The goal was to make a simulation as real as possible..

The simulation takes place in a virtual environment where animals coexist. Each animal will have a set of genes that determines its characteristics and behaviour. As the animals interact with the environment, searching for food, and water and reproducing, their "success" will be evaluated. The more successful animals will be more likely to reproduce, passing on their genes to the next generation. Over time, animal populations are expected to adapt to the environment, developing characteristics that allow them to survive and thrive.

What I did

What I did In this project, I programmed the main animal behaviours like moving, eating and drinking as well as the evolutionary algorithms. I also did all the dressing and animation implementation
