Shelley Manor

Unreal Engine

Third person puzzle game, with fixed cameras in the style of the old resident evil games, made in UE 5.2 by a multidisciplinary team of 8 programmers, 7 artists and 3 game designers (10 months | September 2023 - June 2024).

Find your way through a puzzle-filled manor while barely dodging human eating monsters. You wake up in a dark room with one main objective: escape those sharpened teeth. However you shall not worry, you will meet friends along the way.

What I did

In this project, I was primarily responsible for most of the game interface, some interactives, sound implementation and task support to the rest of the programmers.
