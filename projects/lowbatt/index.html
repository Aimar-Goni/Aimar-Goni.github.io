Low Batt

Bullet hell top-down shooter

Project Overview

Low Batt is a classic bullet hell top-down shooter developed in Unreal Engine 5, published on Steam on July 2023. As our biggest university project, I worked alongside seven game artists, two game designers and seven game programmers.

In this game you play as Andy the robot, where you have to explore a futuristic sci-fi factory and beat all the enemies you encounter to save your robot pet friend.

My Contributions

  • AI Programming - Developed enemy behaviors and combat systems
  • Custom Movement - Implemented evasion algorithms for dynamic combat
  • Animation Systems - Created and integrated character animations
  • Particle Effects - Designed visual effects for weapons and abilities
